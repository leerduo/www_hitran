HITRANonline: news

10 December 2012

Welcome to HITRANonline, an online web-based service for querying the HITRAN database. The current status of the database components offered by this service is as follows:

  • Line-by-line data: HITRAN2008, including updates to February 2012.
  • IR cross sections: HITRAN2008, without updates.
  • UV cross sections: no data at present.
  • Aerosol refractive indices: no data at present.
  • Collision-induced absorption cross sections: as released in September 2011.
